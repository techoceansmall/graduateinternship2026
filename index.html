About the Program

Why We Created
This Program

The OceansMall Graduate Internship & Training Program was established to provide graduates and early-career professionals with meaningful exposure to real business operations while helping them identify career paths that align with their strengths, interests, and aspirations.

Rather than assigning individuals to positions solely based on availability, the program is designed to help participants discover where they can create the greatest impact and achieve long-term professional growth.

The program bridges the gap between academic education, national service, and full-time employment by offering practical experience, mentorship, structured learning, and exposure to real industry challenges.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before submitting your application.

Who can apply for the program?

▼

The program is open to recent university graduates, current National Service Personnel, individuals who have completed national service, and young professionals with 0–3 years of experience. We welcome applicants from all academic backgrounds including technology, business, fisheries, agriculture, logistics, and related fields.

Is this a paid internship?

▼

Compensation details are discussed during the selection process. National Service Personnel receive their standard government stipend. Other participants may receive allowances based on their role and duration. Our primary focus is on providing meaningful experience, mentorship, and career development that creates long-term value.

How long is the program?

▼

The program runs on a flexible timeline. National Service Personnel typically complete the program over their 12-month national service period. Short-term interns engage for 3–6 months. The duration depends on the participant's goals and program needs, and is discussed during the application and selection process.

Can National Service Personnel apply?

▼

Yes! National Service Personnel are strongly encouraged to apply. OceansMall actively accepts NSPs as part of this program. If selected, OceansMall will work with the relevant authorities to facilitate your posting. We believe the national service period is an ideal time to gain structured, meaningful professional experience.

Is employment guaranteed after completing the program?

▼

Employment is not guaranteed, but high-performing participants who demonstrate strong alignment with our values, culture, and business needs are seriously considered for full-time positions as they become available. Many past participants have gone on to join the OceansMall team. The program is also designed to make you a stronger candidate for roles across the industry.

Can participants switch departments during the program?

▼

Yes — this is actually a key feature of the program. The rotational stage is specifically designed to expose participants to multiple departments before specialization. If after your rotation you express strong interest in a different area, we work with you and the relevant teams to accommodate your preference where possible.
